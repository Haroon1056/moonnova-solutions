Do I need a lot of data to use AI?

It depends on the problem. Some AI applications need lots of data, others can work with smaller datasets. We'll assess your data during discovery and recommend approaches that fit what you have. Sometimes we can start with rule-based systems and add ML later as data grows.

How is this different from regular software?

Regular software follows explicit rules written by programmers. AI learns patterns from data and can handle situations you haven't explicitly programmed for. It's better for tasks like understanding language, making predictions, or personalizing experiences.

How much does an AI project cost?

It varies widely based on complexity. A simple chatbot might start around $15k-25k. A custom predictive model could range from $30k-60k. We'll give you a clear estimate after understanding your specific problem and requirements.

How long does it take to build an AI solution?

A basic MVP can be ready in 2-3 months. More complex projects with custom models take 4-6 months. We typically start with a proof of concept to validate the approach before building a full solution.

Will AI replace my employees?

No. We build AI to handle repetitive tasks and augment your team's capabilities, not replace them. The goal is to free your people up for higher-value work that requires human judgment and creativity.

Do I need a data science team to maintain it?

Not necessarily. We build solutions that your existing team can use and maintain. We also offer ongoing support and model retraining as part of our maintenance packages. You don't need to hire data scientists to benefit from AI.
