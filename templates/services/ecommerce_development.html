Contact Us

How much does an e‑commerce site cost?

It depends on complexity. A straightforward store with a standard platform might start around £8k–12k. A custom build with more features will be more. We’re happy to give you a clear estimate after a quick chat about what you need.

Shopify, WooCommerce, or custom?

We don’t push one platform. Shopify is great for getting started quickly. WooCommerce works well if you’re already on WordPress. Custom makes sense when you need something specific. We’ll help you choose the right fit.

How long does it take?

A simple store can be ready in 4–6 weeks. More complex projects take longer. We’ll give you a realistic timeline upfront and keep you updated as we go.

Will I be able to update it myself?

Yes. We build with content management in mind you’ll be able to add products, update prices, and manage orders without needing to code. We’ll also show you how it works.

What happens after launch?

We don’t disappear. We offer ongoing support and maintenance if you need it or we can just be here if you have questions later. It’s up to you.
