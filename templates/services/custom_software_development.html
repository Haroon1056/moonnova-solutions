Why Invest in Custom Software?

Off-the-shelf software is built for everyone, which means it fits no one perfectly. You end up adapting your processes to the tool, rather than the other way around.

Custom software is built around your specific workflows. It grows with your business, integrates with the tools you already use, and eliminates the manual work that slows your team down.

When you invest in custom development, you get a solution that actually solves your problems, without paying for features you don't need or fighting against limitations you didn't expect.

Get in touch

How much does custom software cost?

It depends on complexity. A simple internal tool might start around $15k-25k, while a larger web application can range from $40k-100k+. We'll give you a detailed estimate after understanding your requirements. No ballpark guesses, just honest numbers.

How long does development take?

A basic MVP can be ready in 2-3 months. More complex projects take 4-6 months or longer. We break projects into phases so you can launch core features sooner and add more later.

Do I need a technical background to work with you?

Not at all. We explain things in plain language and guide you through every decision. You focus on your business, and we handle the technical side.

Will I own the code and IP?

Yes. You own everything we build. The code, designs, and intellectual property are 100% yours. No licensing fees, no hidden costs.

What happens after launch?

We offer ongoing support and maintenance packages to keep your software running smoothly. You can also choose to have us train your internal team to manage updates themselves.

Do you build mobile apps too?

We focus on web applications that work perfectly on mobile browsers. If you need native iOS or Android apps, we can recommend trusted partners or discuss hybrid approaches.
