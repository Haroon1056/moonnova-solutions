How long does SEO take to show results?

SEO is a long-term investment. You might see initial improvements in 3-6 months, but significant results typically take 6-12 months. It depends on your competition, current site health, and how much work is needed. We're honest about timelines from the start.

Do you guarantee #1 rankings?

No. Anyone who guarantees #1 rankings is promising something they can't control. Google's algorithm is complex and constantly changing. We guarantee that we'll use proven, ethical strategies to improve your visibility and traffic, but we can't guarantee specific rankings.

What's included in an SEO audit?

Our SEO audit covers technical issues (crawl errors, site speed, mobile friendliness), on-page optimization (meta tags, headers, content), backlink profile, keyword opportunities, and competitor analysis. You get a detailed report with prioritized recommendations.

How much does SEO cost?

SEO pricing varies based on your goals, competition, and how much work is needed. Monthly retainers typically range from $1,500-$4,000 for ongoing SEO. One-time audits and project work are also available. We'll give you a clear proposal after understanding your needs.

Do I need SEO if I run a local business?

Absolutely. Local SEO helps you show up when people search for services in your area. Optimizing your Google Business Profile, getting local reviews, and building local citations can bring in customers who are actively looking for what you offer nearby.

What's the difference between SEO and Google Ads?

SEO drives organic (free) traffic over time, while Google Ads brings paid traffic immediately. SEO is a long-term investment that compounds, while ads stop when you stop paying. Most successful businesses use both. We can help you decide what mix makes sense for your goals and budget.
