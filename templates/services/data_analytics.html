Common Questions About Data & Analytics

Get in touch

Do I need to have clean data before we start?

No. We're used to working with messy, real-world data. Part of our service is cleaning and preparing your data so it can be analyzed. We'll let you know what's possible with your current data and what improvements might help.

How is this different from hiring a data analyst?

We build systems that work for you long-term, not just one-off reports. We set up automated dashboards that update themselves, so you don't need to keep coming back for new analysis. You get ongoing insights without ongoing costs.

What if my data is in different systems?

That's common. We specialize in pulling data from multiple sources databases, spreadsheets, CRMs, analytics tools and combining it into one view. You don't need to move everything to one system.

How much does an analytics project cost?

It depends on complexity. A basic dashboard might start around $8k-12k. A more comprehensive BI solution with multiple data sources could range from $15k-30k. We'll give you a clear estimate after understanding your needs.

How long does it take to build a dashboard?

A simple dashboard can be ready in 3-4 weeks. More complex projects with multiple data sources and custom visualizations take 6-8 weeks. We'll give you a timeline after the discovery phase.

Will I be able to update the dashboard myself?

Yes. We build dashboards that your team can use without technical training. If you need to add new metrics or data sources later, we can help with that. We also offer training sessions for your team.
