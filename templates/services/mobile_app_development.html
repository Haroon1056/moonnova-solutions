Why React Native

Cross-Platform Development Without Compromise

React Native and Expo let us build apps that look and feel native on both iOS and Android, using a single codebase. You get all the benefits of native development without the cost of building and maintaining two separate apps.

Updates and new features ship to both platforms at the same time. Bug fixes go live everywhere instantly. And your users get a consistent experience no matter what device they use.

With Expo, we also get access to a huge library of pre-built components and services, which means faster development and fewer bugs. Camera access, push notifications, payments, and maps all work out of the box.

How is React Native different from building separate native apps?

With React Native, we write one codebase that works on both iOS and Android. Native apps require separate teams, separate codebases, and double the development time. React Native apps perform like native apps but cost less and launch faster.

What is Expo and why do you use it?

Expo is a framework that sits on top of React Native and provides pre-built modules for common features like cameras, notifications, and maps. It speeds up development significantly and lets us push updates instantly without going through app store approval.

How much does a mobile app cost?

A simple MVP might start around $20k-30k. More complex apps with custom features can range from $40k-80k. We'll give you a clear estimate after understanding your requirements. No vague ballpark figures.

How long does it take to build an app?

An MVP can be ready in 2-4 months. More feature-rich apps take 4-6 months. Because we build for both platforms simultaneously, you're saving 40-50% of the time it would take to build two separate native apps.

Will my app feel native on both iOS and Android?

Yes. React Native compiles to native code, so the performance and feel are native. We also follow platform-specific design guidelines so your app feels right at home on both iOS and Android devices.

Do you help with app store submission?

Absolutely. We handle the entire app store submission process for both Apple App Store and Google Play Store, including preparing screenshots, writing descriptions, and navigating the review process.
