Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2024 MoonNova Solutions

1. Introduction

MoonNova Solutions ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you:

  • Visit our website
  • Contact us
  • Use our services
  • Engage with us for web, software, AI, analytics, or marketing solutions

By using our website or services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate our business and provide services.

A. Personal Information You Provide

When you contact us or work with us, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company name
  • Project details
  • Billing information (if applicable)

If you apply for a job or partnership, we may collect:

  • Resume/CV
  • Professional background
  • Contact information

B. Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages
  • Referral source

This information helps us improve website performance and user experience.

C. Project-Related Information

For client projects, we may process:

  • Business requirements
  • Technical documentation
  • Data provided by clients for development purposes

We process such information strictly for project execution.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

Service Delivery

To develop, maintain, and deliver web, software, AI, analytics, and marketing solutions.

Communication

To respond to inquiries, provide project updates, and share important information.

Business Operations

To manage contracts, invoices, and internal processes.

Website Improvement

To analyze website usage and improve performance and content.

Legal Compliance

To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal information.

We may share information only in the following cases:

Service Providers

We may use trusted third-party services such as:

Hosting providers Payment processors Analytics tools Communication platforms

These providers are required to protect your data.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

With Your Consent

We may share information if you explicitly authorize us to do so.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including:

Secure hosting environments
Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
Restricted access to client data
Regular system updates

While we take appropriate precautions, no system can guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary:

  • For active projects
  • For legal and accounting requirements
  • To maintain business records

Once no longer required, data is securely deleted.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

Access your personal data
Correct inaccurate information
Request deletion of your data
Withdraw consent for communications

To exercise your rights, contact us at:

info@moonnova-solutions.com

We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies to:

  • Improve website functionality
  • Analyze traffic
  • Enhance user experience

You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

Learn how to manage cookies in: Chrome Firefox Safari Edge

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites.

We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

We encourage you to review their policies separately.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed toward individuals under 16 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided information, please contact us and we will remove it.

11. International Users

If you are accessing our website from outside our country of operation, please note that your information may be processed in our operating location.

By using our services, you consent to such processing.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
