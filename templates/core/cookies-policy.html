Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2024 MoonNova Solutions

This policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website.

They help websites:

  • Function properly
  • Improve user experience
  • Analyze traffic
  • Remember user preferences

By using our website, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

2. How We Use Cookies

MoonNova Solutions uses cookies to:

  • Ensure website functionality
  • Analyze website performance
  • Improve user experience
  • Monitor traffic and engagement

We do not use cookies to sell your data.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

A. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly.

They enable:

  • Page navigation
  • Form submissions
  • Security features

Without these cookies, some parts of the website may not work correctly.

B. Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or similar services) to:

  • Understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Track page views
  • Measure performance

These cookies collect aggregated, non-personal information.

C. Performance & Optimization Cookies

These cookies help us:

  • Improve website speed
  • Test design changes
  • Enhance functionality

They help us deliver a better browsing experience.

D. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services used on our website, such as:

Hosting providers Embedded tools Analytics platforms

These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies.

4. How Long Cookies Stay on Your Device

Cookies may be:

Session Cookies

Deleted when you close your browser

Temporary

Persistent Cookies

Stored for a defined period to remember preferences

Permanent

Retention periods vary depending on the type of cookie.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Most browsers allow you to:

  • Delete cookies
  • Block cookies
  • Set preferences for specific websites
Learn how to manage cookies in: Chrome Firefox Safari Edge

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.
